Frequently Asked Questions

1. How do I place a bid?

Go to the Featured Items section, select an item, and click ‚ÄúBid Now‚Äù. You can raise or cancel your bid anytime before the auction ends.

2. How do I recharge my account?

Open the Recharge Page, select your preferred amount in units, choose a payment method, and click Confirm Recharge.

3. What happens if I win an auction?

If you win, the item will appear in your Items Won section, and the corresponding units will be deducted from your account balance.

4. How can I view my bid history?

Your Account Page shows a list of all your active, won, and lost bids for easy tracking.

5. Who do I contact for support?

You can reach out via support@kunstconnoisseur.com or through our in-site message form on the Contact page.
